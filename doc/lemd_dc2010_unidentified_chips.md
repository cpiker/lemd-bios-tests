# LEMD DC-2010 -- Unidentified Chip Part Numbers

132 of 193 chips have U-numbers confirmed from the PCB silkscreen but
their chip part numbers have not been read and recorded. These are
almost entirely 74LS/74S-series TTL glue logic. The U-number positions
are correct (verified from the physical board) but the specific part at
each position needs to be read from the chip markings.

To complete the inventory: read the first line of text on each chip
package (e.g. "SN74LS244N") and record it next to the U-number below.
